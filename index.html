About Me

Hi, I’m Kartik Chandra, an aspiring author and storyteller currently studying in Class 8 at Bhartiya Vidya Bhavan Mehta Vidyalaya. Writing has been my passion for as long as I can remember, and I love crafting stories that blend mystery, adventure, and the thrill of the unknown. Through my writing, I aim to take readers on unforgettable journeys, sparking their imagination and keeping them on the edge of their seats. My current project, Battle of Starlight, is a series that explores the secrets of time travel and how a single object can connect generations through history. When I’m not writing, I enjoy reading, exploring fascinating historical events, and finding inspiration in everyday life. Join me on this exciting adventure as I continue to create and share stories that will leave you guessing till the very end!
